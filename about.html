About

Team, motivation, and background behind StereoPep.

Motivation

Peptide drugs are an important and growing class of therapeutics. However, machine learning models for peptide property prediction often ignore stereochemistry, treating L- and D-amino acid variants as identical. In reality, stereoisomers can have profoundly different biological activities, stability, and pharmacokinetic profiles.

StereoPep was created to close this gap — providing a rigorous benchmark that forces models to account for chirality when predicting peptide properties. We hope it will drive the development of stereoisomer-aware molecular representations and models.
